The Night Drive

Dec 29 • Evening (6 PM)

Route

Hyderabad → Hampi (370 km)

Food to Eat

The Boulder City

2

Ruins of Hampi

Dec 30 • 7 AM - 5 PM

Places to Visit

  • • Virupaksha Temple: The living temple.
  • • Vithala Temple: Famous Stone Chariot.
  • • Lotus Mahal: Indo-Islamic architecture.
  • • Elephant Stables: Royal shelters.

Experiences

  • • Coracle Ride: Cross the Tungabhadra river in a round basket boat.
  • • Matanga Hill: Hike up for the best sunset view in Hampi.

Taste of Hampi

Lunch at The Mango Tree. Try the Thali and Banoffee Pie. Look for street-side Paddu (rice balls) for breakfast.

Stay Evolve Back Hampi / Gopi Guest House

Royal Mysore

Dec 31 • New Year's Eve

Highlights

  • Chikmagalur Drive: Coffee estates en route.
  • Mysore Palace: 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM visit.
  • Chamundi Hill: Blessings at 4:00 PM.
  • Brindavan Gardens: Musical Fountain Light Show.

Food to Eat

Mylari Dosa for breakfast. Rao's Mess for a leaf-meal lunch. Buy Mysore Pak from Guru Sweets.

Stay Radisson Blu / Royal Orchid Metropole

Queen of Hills

Jan 1 • Happy New Year

Visit

  • • Botanical Gardens: Exotic flora.
  • • Doddabetta Peak: Highest point in Nilgiris.
  • • Tea Factory: See how tea is made.

Do

  • • Boating: Morning ride at Ooty Lake.
  • • Market Stroll: Buy homemade chocolates & eucalyptus oil.

Taste of Ooty

Lunch at Shinkows (classic Chinese). Grab some Ooty Varkey (crusty biscuits) and Hot Chocolate near the lake.

Stay Sterling Ooty Elk Hill / Sinclairs Retreat

The Spiritual Stop

Jan 2 • Ooty to Coimbatore

Coonoor (Morning)

Visit Sim's Park, Lamb's Rock, and Dolphin's Nose for breathtaking views of the Nilgiris.

Isha Foundation

Visit the massive Adiyogi Statue (112 ft). Sit in silence at the Dhyanalinga.

Tip: Try the healthy sattvic food at Isha Cafeteria.

Stay The Residency / Hotel Vivanta (Coimbatore)

The Scotland of India

Jan 3 & 4 • Coorg

Day 6: Nature & Sunsets

  • • Raja's Seat: A garden with a spectacular sunset view.
  • • Abbey Falls: A roaring waterfall amidst coffee bushes.

Day 7: Elephants & Origins

  • • Dubare Elephant Camp: Watch and bathe elephants (8-11 AM).
  • • Talacauvery: The origin of the river Cauvery.
  • • Coffee Walk: Guided tour inside a plantation.

Kodava Cuisine

Must try: Pandi Curry (Pork) or Bamboo Shoot Curry with Akki Roti (Rice Roti). Drink fresh Coorg Coffee.

Stay Taj Madikeri / Coorg Cliffs Resort
